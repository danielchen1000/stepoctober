Frequently Asked Questions

How are steps counted?

Steps are collected weekly via the submission form linked above. Please use data from your phone's health app (Apple Health, Google Fit) or your fitness tracker (Fitbit, Garmin, etc.).

How are y'all verifying steps? Seems easily cheatable.

Steps are verified with a screenshot from your step tracker. The screenshot must clearly show the dates and your total step count for that week. Please make sure the data is easy to read. Submissions without a clear screenshot won't be counted. We're keeping this a friendly competition, so we're operating on the honor system!

When are the rankings updated?

The leaderboard is updated every Monday with the previous week's results. Be sure to submit your steps before the deadline!
